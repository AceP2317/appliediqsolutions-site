This site already shows what I build. This page shows the build as it happens.

Field notes collects everything in one stream: posts written here, what I share on LinkedIn and Nextdoor, and — as the practice grows — what other people say about the work. Every syndicated entry links back to the original conversation, so you can always see it where it was said.

Why bother? Because the whole pitch of this practice is working software over slideware, and a build log is the honest version of marketing: dated, specific, and public. The tools themselves are on the [Work page](/work/), running live in your browser. This is the running commentary.
